Mythos by Stephen Fry

Although familiar in general with many Greek myths, I have never really read them in depth. This was an enjoyable dive into Greek mythology especially with Fry’s excellent narration. Those Greeks were wild!

isbn-13: 9780718188726

Audible

Publisher’s Description: “The Greek myths are amongst the greatest stories ever told, passed down through millennia and inspiring writers and artists as varied as Shakespeare, Michelangelo, James Joyce and Walt Disney. They are embedded deeply in the traditions, tales and cultural DNA of the West. You’ll fall in love with Zeus, marvel at the birth of Athena, wince at Cronus and Gaia’s revenge on Ouranos, weep with King Midas and hunt with the beautiful and ferocious Artemis. Spellbinding, informative and moving, Stephen Fry’s Mythos perfectly captures these stories for the modern age - in all their rich and deeply human relevance.”
