Good Behavior by Blake Crouch

Blake Crouch is becoming a favorite author. I really enjoyed Recursion and Dark Matter. Unlike those two there is no element of science fiction here, but a still enjoyable ride alongside ex-thief Letty Dobesh and her adventures.

Publisher’s Description: “Three interlocking novellas details the escapades of ex-thief Letty Dobesh as she uses her skills to stop other crimes from taking place.”
