Ready Player One by Ernest Cline

isbn-13: 9780307887436

Audible

I really enjoyed this trip into the ‘metaverse’ along with all its 1980’s nostalgia. It’s not high art, but it was fun.

Don’t bother with the movie though, it’s rubbish.

Publisher’s Description: “Immersing himself in a mid-21st-century technological virtual utopia to escape an ugly real world of famine, poverty and disease, Wade Watts joins an increasingly violent effort to solve a series of puzzles by the virtual world’s super-wealthy creator, who has promised that the winner will be his heir. (This book was previously listed in Forecast.)”
