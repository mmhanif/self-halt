The Sidekicks Initiative: A Comedy Superhero Adventure by Barry Hutchinson

ASIN: B07N6MQZCG

Audible

A fun listen. The premise is a world where superheroes regularly save the planet from supervillains. They used to have sidekicks with greatly diminished powers compared to the superheroes, but the sidekicks have gone into retirement and are living ordinary lives. That is until a new Uber-villain arrives and wipeout the superheroes - now its up to the sidekicks to save the day.

Its not going to change your life, but still quite enjoyable. In a similar category to the Orlando People and Andrea Vernon series - interesting twists on old comicbook premises with a heavy dose of sardonic humor.
